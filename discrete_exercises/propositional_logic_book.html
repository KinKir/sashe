Исказно сметање (Propositional Logic)

Дефениција

Вежба 1. Дали се искази следниве реченици:
a) Boston is the capital of Massachusetts.
b) Miami is the capital of Florida.
c) 2 + 3 = 5
d) 5 + 7 = 10
e) x + 2 = 11
f) Answer this question

Решение

a) да (вистинит исказ)

b) да (невистинит исказ - Tallahassee е главен град)

c) да (вистинит исказ)

d) да (невистинит исказ - 12 е точен исказ)

e) не (не се искажува факт вредноста на вистиноста зависи од променливата `x`)

f) не (не се искажува факт)

Негaција на исказ

Вежба 3. Што е нагација на следните искази?
а) Today is Thursday.
b) There is no pollution in New Jersey.
c) 2 + 1 = 3.
d) The sumer in Maine is hot and sunny.

Решение

a) Today isn't Thursday.

b) There is pollution in New Jersey.

c) `2 + 1 != 3`

d) It is not the case that the summer in Maine is hot and sunny.

d) Diane did not ride her bike 100 miles on Sunday.

Вежба 6. Suppose that Smartphone A has 256 MB RAM and 32 GB ROM, and the resolution of its camera is 8 MP; Smartphone B has 288 MB RAM and 64 GB ROM, and the resolution of its camera is 4 MP; and Smartphone C has 128 MB RAM and 32 GB ROM, and the resolution of its camera is 5 MP. Determine the truth value of each of these propositions
a) Smartphone B has the most RAM of these three smartphones.
b) Smartphone C has more ROM or a higher resolution camera than Smartphone B.
c) Smartphone B has more RAM, more ROM, and a higher resolution camera than Smartphone A
d) If Smartphone B has more RAM and more ROM than Smartphone C, then it also has a higher resolution camera.
e) Smartphone A has more RAM than Smartphone B if and only if Smartphone B has more RAM than Smartphone A.

Решение

a) Станува збор за исказ `p`: Smartphone B has the most RAM of these three smartphones.
`T` ( 288 MB> 256 > 128MB)

b) `T` (5 MP > 4 MP)

c) `F` (4 MP < 8 MP)

d) `F` (Smartfone A have higher resolution)

e) `F`

Вежба 7. Suppose that during the most recent fiscal year, the annual revenue of Acme Computer was 138 billion dollars and its net profit was 8 billion dollars, the annual revenue of Nadir Software was 87 billion dollars and its net profit was 5 billion dollars, and the annual revenue of Quixote Media was 111 billion dollars and its net profit was 13 billion dollars. Determine the truth value of each of these propositions for the most recent fiscal year
a) Quixote Media had the largest annual revenue.
b) Nadir Software had the lowest net profit and Acme Computer had the largest annual revenue
c) Acme Computer had the largest net profit or Quixote Media had the largest net profit.
d) If Quixote Media had the smallest net profit, then Acme Computer had the largest annual revenue.
e) Nadir Software had the smallest net profit if and only if Acme Computer had the largest annual revenue.

Решение

a) `F` (Исказ каде вредноста на вистиноста е неточно бидејки Acme има најголем годишен обрт)

b) `T` (Станува збор за конјукција каде двата искази имаат вредност на вистиноста точен, па според табелата за вистиност вредноста на вистиноста е точен)

c) `T` (Станува збор за дисјункција каде првиот исказ има вредност на вистиност неточно а вредноста на вистинитост на вториот исказ е точно па според табелата на вистиност за дисјункција вредноста на вистиноста на дисјункцијата е точно.)

d) `T` (Станува збор за условен исказ каде хипотезата (првиот исказ) има вредност на вистиност неточно а заклучокот (вториот исказ) има вредност на вистинитост точно. Според табелата на вредност на вистиноста овој условен исказ има вредност точно.

e) `T` (Станува збор за еквиваленција каде двата искази имаат вредност точно, па според табелата за вредност на вистиноста вредноста на еквиваленцијата е точно.)

Вежба 9.Let`p` and `q` be the propositions “Swimming at the New Jersey shore is allowed” and “Sharks have been spotted near the shore,” respectively. Express each of these compound propositions as an English sentence
a) `not q`
b) `p ^^ q`
c) `not p vv q`
d) `p -> not q`
e) `not q -> p`
f) `not p -> not q`
g) `p harr not q`
h) `not p (p vv not q)`

Решение

a) Sharks have not been spotted near the shore.

b) Swimming at the New Jersey shore is allowed, and sharks have been spotted near the shore.

c) Swimming at the New Jersey shore is not allowed, or sharks have been spotted near the shore.

d) If swimming at the New Jersey shore is allowed, then sharks have not been spotted near the shore.

e) If sharks have not been spotted near the shore, then swimming at the New Jersey shore is allowed.

f) If swimming at the New Jersey shore is not allowed, then sharks have not been spotted near the shore.

g) Swimming at the New Jersey shore is allowed if and only if sharks have not been spotted near the shore.

h) Swimming at the New Jersey shore is not allowed, and either swimming at the New Jersey shore is allowed or sharks have not been spotted near the shore.

Вежба 10.Let `p` and `q` be the propositions “The election is decided” and “The votes have been counted,” respectively. Express each of these compound propositions as an English sentence.
a) `not p`
b) `p vv q`
c) `not p ^^ q`
d) `q -> p`
e) `not q -> not p`
f) `not p -> not q`
g) `p harr q`
h) `not q vv (not p ^^ q)`

Решение

a) The election isn't decided.

b) The election is decided or the votes have been counted.

c) The election isn't decided and the votes have been counted.

d) If the votes have been counted then the election is decided.

e) If the votes haven't been counted then the election isn't decided.

f) If election isn't decided then the votes haven't been counted.

g) The election is decided if and only if the votes have been counted.

h) If the votes haven't been counted or the election isn't decided and and the votes have been counted.

`p` : Температурата е под нула.
`q` : Врне снег
Запиши ги следните искази користејки ги `p` и `q` и логичките сврзници.
a) Температурата е под нула и врне снег.
b) Температурата е под нула но не врне снег.
c) Температурата не е под нула и не врне снег.
d) It is either snowing or below freezing (or both).
e) If it is below freezing, it is also snowing.

Решение

a) Конјукција - `p ^^ q`.

b) Конјукција од `p` со негација од `q` - `p ^^ not q`.

c) Конјукција - `not p ^^ not q`

d) If the votes have been counted then the election is decided.

e) If the votes haven't been counted then the election isn't decided.

f) If election isn't decided then the votes haven't been counted.

g) The election is decided if and only if the votes have been counted.

h) If the votes haven't been counted or the election isn't decided and and the votes have been counted.

Исказот е декларативна реченица (т.е. реченица која изнесува факт) кој е точен или неточен но не и двете.

Негација од исказот `p`, означена со `not p` (или означен со `overset (-)(p)`), е исказот
„Не е случај дека `p`“
Исказот `not p` се чита „not `p`.“ Логичката вредност на негацијата од `p`, `not p`, e спротивна на логичкат вредност на `p`.
